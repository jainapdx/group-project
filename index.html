About

Janet Majors has been providing massage therapy relief for six years. She's passionate about working with her clients closely until they reach their ultimate state of relaxation. Janet keeps up to date with new massage therapy techniques. She graduated from the Massage Therapy School of Portland in 2007 and continues for yearly training. She also attends the Massage Therapy Relief for American trade show every year.

Offerings

We are please to offer the following services:

Deep Tissue Massage

This massage goes beyond the surface of the skin and underlying soft tissue to deep areas of the muscle and right to the “belly” of the muscle.

$90 per hour

Reiki Massage

A Japanese technique for stress reduction and relaxation that promotes healing. Reiki is powerful and simple.

$90 per hour

Shiatsu Massage

This technique incorporates the pressure of fingers, palms, elbows, knees, and feet. It unblocks the flow of life energy at specific acupuncture points and restores balance.

$90 per hour

Sports Massage

Used as a preventive medicine for many athletes before, during, and after sporting events. Provides relaxation before an athletic event.

$120 per hour

Hot Stone Massage

An incredible massage that gives your blood vessels an ebb and flow affect (hot and cold).

$150 per hour

Swedish Massage

Combines several techniques including kneading, rolling, effleurage, petrissage, friction, percussive and taping strokes to aid in stress and tension reduction and remove toxic wastes.

$175 per hour
